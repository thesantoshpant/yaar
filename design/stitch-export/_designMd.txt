## Brand & Style
This design system is crafted to feel like a "smart friend"—a supportive, reliable companion for South Asian students navigating the complexities of academic and personal life. The aesthetic blends **Corporate Modern** structure with **Soft Minimalist** warmth to ensure the interface never feels like an intimidating consultancy or a clinical institution.

The emotional response should be one of safety and optimism. By utilizing a "warm-tech" approach, the UI balances high-functioning AI utility with human-centric softness. It prioritizes clarity, honesty, and hope, using a mix of generous negative space and tasteful, culturally resonant accents.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in the vibrant yet comforting tones of South Asian heritage, reimagined for a modern digital experience. 

- **Primary (Marigold/Saffron):** Used for key actions and brand moments to evoke energy and wisdom.
- **Secondary (Deep Teal):** Provides a grounding, trustworthy contrast to the primary gold; used for navigation and depth.
- **Background (Warm Cream):** Replaces harsh whites to reduce eye strain and create a "paper-like" tactile comfort.
- **Accent (Coral Pink):** Reserved for high-energy interactions, highlights, and micro-expressions.
- **Text (Near-black Plum):** Offers high legibility while maintaining the warm temperature of the overall palette.

## Typography
The typography strategy pairs the geometric confidence of **Plus Jakarta Sans** for headings with the contemporary readability of **Be Vietnam Pro** for body text. 

Headlines should be set with tight tracking and a bold weight to mimic the "Clash Display" aesthetic, creating a youthful and assertive presence. Body text is prioritized for comfort, utilizing a generous line height (1.5–1.6) to ensure that even long counseling responses remain digestible. Use "headline-xl" sparingly for hero sections and "headline-md" for card titles.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout follows a **Fluid Grid** model with an emphasis on generous margins to avoid a cluttered or "busy" atmosphere. 

- **Desktop:** 12-column grid, 24px gutters, and 80px side margins.
- **Tablet:** 8-column grid, 24px gutters, and 40px side margins.
- **Mobile:** 4-column grid, 16px gutters, and 24px side margins.

Content should be grouped into logical "islands" (cards) with ample padding (`md` or 24px) inside each container to ensure elements have room to breathe. Avoid cramming information; if a screen feels tight, increase the vertical spacing (`lg`) between sections.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth in this design system is achieved through **Tonal Layers** and **Ambient Shadows** rather than stark physical offsets. 

Surfaces are primarily defined by the background (`#FFF6E9`). Raised elements like cards use a very soft, multi-layered shadow with a subtle tint of the Near-black Plum (`#1A1A2E`) at low opacity (3-5%). 

To reinforce the "Warm Buddy" feel, all cards and containers must feature a subtle `1px` solid border using a slightly darker version of the cream or the marigold tint (`#FFEFC9`). This "ghost border" provides structure without the clinical feel of a standard gray stroke.

## Shapes
The shape language is defined by **Generous Curves**. Avoid sharp 90-degree corners entirely. 

Standard components (Cards, Input Fields) use a base radius of `16px`. Larger containers or featured sections should scale up to `24px` (`rounded-xl`). Interactive elements like buttons and chips should utilize **Pill-shaped** (fully rounded) geometry to emphasize the approachable, friendly nature of the AI counselor.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Pill-shaped, Marigold background, Plum text. Bold weight.
- **Secondary:** Pill-shaped, Deep Teal background, Cream text.
- **Ghost:** Pill-shaped, no background, 1px Plum or Teal border.

### Input Fields
- Background should be a slightly lighter tint of the cream or pure white.
- 16px border-radius with a 1px border (`#FFEFC9`).
- Focus state: Border color changes to Marigold with a soft outer glow.

### Cards
- Always use the 16px or 24px corner radius.
- Background: White or very pale Marigold tint.
- Subtle 1px border is mandatory to distinguish from the cream background.

### Chips & Tags
- Used for student interests or category tags.
- Always pill-shaped.
- Use the Secondary Tint (`#DCEDEC`) with Deep Teal text for a calming effect.

### Interactive Accents
- Use tasteful, high-quality emojis at the start of headers or as illustrative focal points in cards (e.g., 💡 for tips, 🌱 for growth).
- Progress bars should use the Success green (`#1FA37A`) with rounded end-caps.
